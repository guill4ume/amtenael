# Class Library: Warlock

Originating from a cult that lived underground in caves beneath the Realm of Midgard, Warlocks are cloth-wearing worshippers of Hel who have mastered the art of spell weaving. They have three types of spells: Primary spells, Secondary spells, and Utility spells.

Warlock Primary spells take about twice as long to cast as regular spells in the game &#8211; however, anytime the Warlock casts a primary spell, they can (at no cost) add in a secondary spell that will land at the same time as the primary. This allows Warlocks to mix and match pairs of spells to fit whatever situation they find themselves in.
