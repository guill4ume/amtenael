# Class Library: Valewalker

Valewalkers are cloth-wearing Hibernian mages who are given the ability to wield a large Scythe as a weapon. They are the strong arm of the Sylvan community &#8211; they are the primary fighters of this natural magic-using people. Valewalkers wear mage-like cloth robes, which they can augment by casting armor-absorption and armor factor self-buffing spells. They also can evade and parry attacks.

Valewalker combat styles center around their magical nature, and augment their scythe&#8217;s damage with magical effects.
