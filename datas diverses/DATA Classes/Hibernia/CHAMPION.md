# Class Library: Champion

Champions are a fusion between a Caster and a Fighter. They have offensive capabilities, but they are enhanced through their powerful debuff spells. Champions instantly fling debuffs upon their enemies, which greatly hinder the enemy&#8217;s ability to fight at his best level. This ultimately gives the Champion the upper hand in many situations.

They have defensive abilities including evasion and protection skills. Their Scale armor helps protect them from any further damage that is dealt from enemy swings. Though their debuffs are the highlight of the Valor spell line, Champions also have a self-only buff, snare spell, and instant direct damage shout.
